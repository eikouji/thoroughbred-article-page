Thoroughbred Horse

Thoroughbred horses are tall, strong horses that are utilized in many sports such as racing, jumping, polo matches, and dressage.

History of the Thoroughbred

The Thoroughbred breed was developed in England during the 17th and 18th centuries by crossbreeding native mares with imported Arabian, Barb, and Turkoman stallions. The resulting breed was known for its speed, agility, and endurance, making it ideal for horse racing.

Temperment of Thoroughbreds

Thoroughbreds are known for their spirited and energetic temperaments. They are intelligent and quick learners, but they can also be sensitive and high-strung. Proper training and handling are essential to bring out the best in these horses.
